This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile app or website. --- ## 1. Information We Collect We may collect the following types of information: - **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number, profile picture, payment details. - **Account Information:** Username, password, account preferences. - **Transaction Information:** Order history, payments, and withdrawals. - **Device Information:** IP address, device model, operating system, browser type. - **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent in the app, and interaction patterns. - **Location Data:** Only if you allow location access in the app. --- ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use your data for: - Creating and managing your GigLynk account. - Connecting clients with freelancers. - Processing payments and withdrawals. - Sending notifications, updates, and promotional offers. - Improving app features and security. - Detecting and preventing fraud or misuse. --- ## 3. How We Share Your Information We **do not** sell your personal data. We may share it with: - **Service Providers:** For payment processing, cloud hosting, and customer support. - **Legal Authorities:** If required by law or to protect GigLynk’s rights. - **Business Transfers:** In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets. --- ## 4. Your Privacy Controls You can: - Update or edit your profile at any time. - Opt out of marketing emails. - Delete your account by contacting our support team. --- ## 5. Data Storage & Security - We store your information securely using industry-standard controls such as encryption and access control. - We keep your data only for as long as necessary for business or legal purposes. --- ## 6.
